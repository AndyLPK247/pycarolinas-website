Saturday, June 20 *

Raleigh, NC

PyCarolinas is back!

Join us for a one-day "unconference" all about Python. Our event will empower the Python community to be awesome right here in the Carolinas.

Lightning Talks

Instead of formal tracks, we will hold 5-10 minute talks all day long! We welcome talks about anything Python-related: software dev, data science, nifty projects, language tricks, etc. Sign up to speak!

Collab Clusters

Want to team up with other Pythonistas to work on a project? Or maybe you want to learn some new skills? We'll set aside tables for folks to collaborate together!

Hallway Track

Python conferences are great because they bring people together. We'll provide open space for everyone to share ideas, show code, and swap stickers.

Venue

PyCarolinas will be held at Red Hat Annex, 190 E Davie St, Raleigh, NC 27601 - right in the heart of downtown Raleigh. We hope to accommodate about 100 (or more) Pythonistas! Lunch will also be provided.

Registration

Registration will open May 1, 2020*. Tickets will be FREE!

* Dates may change due to COVID-19.
