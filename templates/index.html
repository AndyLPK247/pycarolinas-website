SATURDAY, JUNE 20 *

RALEIGH, NC

PyCarolinas is Back!

Join us for an awesome one-day "unconference" all about Python, for the community and by the community.

Lightning Talks

Instead of formal tracks, we will hold 5-10 minute talks all day long! We welcome talks about anything Python-related. Anyone can speak. Sign up!

Collab Clusters

Want to team up with other Pythonistas to work on a project? Or maybe you want to learn some new skills? We'll set aside tables for folks to collaborate together!

Hallway Track

Python conferences are great because they bring people together. We'll provide open space for everyone to share ideas, show code, and swap stickers.

Venue

PyCarolinas will be held at Red Hat Annex, right in the heart of downtown Raleigh. We hope to accommodate about 100 (or more) Pythonistas. Lunch will also be provided. Check our Venue page for details.

Registration

Registration will open May 1, 2020*. Tickets will be FREE!

* Dates may change due to COVID-19.

Contact

Follow us on Twitter at @PyCarolinas, join our Slack room, and sign up for our newsletter!
