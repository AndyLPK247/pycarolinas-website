Sponsors

PyCarolinas 2020 wouldn't be possible without sponsors. Even going lean, we still need the support of a few key sponsors to make our unconference happen. We sincerely thank and recognize each of them!

Recognizing Our Sponsors

  • Venue Sponsor: Red Hat graciously provided our venue, the Red Hat Annex, for free.
  • Website Sponsor: Automation Panda built and hosted the PyCarolinas 2020 website.

Remaining Sponsorship Needs

Below are our remaining sponsorship needs in order of importance. We need both the finances and the volunteers for the following needs. Please contact us if you would like to support PyCarolinas 2020 in any of these ways!

  1. Lunch Sponsor: We need to provide pizza and soft drinks for lunch.
  2. Coffee Sponsor: We would like to provide coffee in the morning.
  3. Signage Sponsor: We would like to print "PyCarolinas 2020" signs to help people find the venue.
  4. Sticker Sponsor: We would like to print and distribute "PyCarolinas 2020" stickers.
  5. Streaming Sponsor: We would like to live stream the lightning talks and then save their recordings on YouTube.
  6. Dinner Sponsor: We invite a sponsor to host an after-conference dinner party at a nearby restaurant for conference attendees.
